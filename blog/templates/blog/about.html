What is this Blog?

This blog is a place where I can post my projects, information about me and blog about my progress as a programmer. Simple as that. Come look around and mess with the gallery interactives!


Why did I make this Blog?

As a starter of something new, it is quite something to provide a new perspective on it. When I was reading the book, Deep Learning for Coders with fastai and PyTorch: AI Applications Without a PhD, I found it interesting to create a blog to show my progress with Machine Learning. I have worked with Sklearn, but it was too simple and I wanted more. In the blog, I can talk about my journey not only for machine learning, but programming as a whole. Not only that, but feature some projects I am proud of.


What is the future of the Blog?

There is more information to be posted here, the blog is lacking in projects and whatnot, given time, it will advance to be much larger. I want it to also provide lessons in the future. I have created a good base for the blog and it will only get better in the future.
