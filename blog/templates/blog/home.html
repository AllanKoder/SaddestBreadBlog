Why do I love Programming?

As a kid, I saw programming as a new way to tell a story, I was fascinated by the idea. I started with the basic idea of making video games, and reached an advanced level. I wanted to learn more about logic. I was fascinated by all the possibilities of technology and how it will change the world. I love programming because I love creating. As time passes, I will only continue to learn more and develop my programming skills.
